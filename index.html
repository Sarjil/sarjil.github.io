About Me

My name is Sarjil Miah, and I am a New York City based Software Engineer. Having a background in
Construction Project Mangagement, I understand the importance of detail orientation,
working in a fast paced environment, and delivering high quality product to exceed client satisfaction.

My passion for technology drove me to taking a life changing risk, and changing careers.
In January 2020, I joined App Academy and took my first steps in learning how to
become a software engineer. Since then, I've gained knowledge in multiple technologies
including Ruby on Rails, JavaScript, React, Redux, SQL, and more. I've created three projects
each utilitizing different technologies to showcase my versatility.

Project List

Last Call

Last Call is a social networking app for beer enthusiasts, inspired by Untappd. Add to the beers and breweries evergrowing database. Share check-ins, reviews, and "toast" a friends post!

Where to
Feast

Where To Feast is an interactive app designed for groups of indecisive people who could not agree on where to eat. Utilizing websockets and Google Map API, invite your friends to a single session where all participants can enter options of what they want to eat. The randomizer will select a choice from the options, and direct you to restaurants based off your current location.

Monster
Card Battle

Monster Card Battle is a gothic-themed card game where the player must use their cards to defeat the opponents cards, and get their health points to zero.
